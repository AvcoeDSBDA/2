About Us

Welcome to Amrutvahini College of Engineering

Amrutvahini College of Engineering (AVCOE), Sangamner established in 1983 under the leadership of Hon. Late. Bhausaheb Thorat and progressing under the guidance of Hon. Shri. Balasaheb Thorat, Ex-Revenue Minister, Government of Maharashtra and Hon. Dr. Sudhir Tambe (Ex-MLC Nashik).

It is spread over 15.6 acres of ample greenery, with four times accreditation by NBA, New Delhi, Graded 'A+' by NAAC Government of India and International Academic Accreditation ISO 9001:2015 by TUV SUD. It boasts state-of-the-art IT centre with 500 MBPs Leased Line and 300 MBPs Broadband Internet Facility.

The college has a vast library housing 66,735 books, 129 print journals and 275 e-journals. AVCOE provides 100% placement assistance through value-added courses, foreign languages (Japanese, German), and training programs. The institute is committed to excellence-driven engineering education for building the future of engineers and entrepreneurs.

Vision

To create opportunities for rural students to become able engineers and technocrats through continual excellence in engineering education.

Mission

Our mission is to create self-disciplined, physically fit, mentally robust and morally strong engineers and technocrats with a high degree of integrity and a sense of purpose. They should be capable of meeting challenges of ever-advancing technology for the benefit of mankind and nature.

We, the management, faculty, and staff, therefore promise to strive hard and commit ourselves to achieve this objective through a continuous process of learning and appreciation of the needs of the time.
